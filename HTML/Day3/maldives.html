About Maldives

The Maldives is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in the world known for its white sand beaches and clear blue water.

Many tourists visit Maldives to enjoy water sports, luxury resorts, and peaceful island life.

Top Attractions

  • Male City
  • Banana Reef
  • Vaadhoo Island

Travel Tip

Best time to visit Maldives is between November and April.
