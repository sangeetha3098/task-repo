Tamil Nadu Election 2026 Result

The Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 became one of the most talked about elections in the state. Many political parties participated and campaigned across Tamil Nadu to gain the support of the people.

Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), led by actor Vijay, created a major impact in the election and emerged as the single largest party by winning 108 seats. The party received strong support from youth, first-time voters, and people expecting political change.

Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) secured 59 seats and continued to remain one of the important political parties in Tamil Nadu. AIADMK won 47 seats and maintained its influence in several regions of the state.

Other parties such as Congress, PMK, CPI, and VCK also participated in the election and won seats in different constituencies. Since no party crossed the majority mark of 118 seats, the election initially resulted in a hung assembly.

Later, TVK received support from alliance parties and independent members, helping the party achieve the required majority to form the government in Tamil Nadu.

After gaining majority support, TVK leader Vijay took oath as the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu in Chennai. His victory marked an important political change in the history of the state.

The Tamil Nadu Election 2026 highlighted the importance of democracy and showed how people’s votes can bring new leadership and political change in the state.
