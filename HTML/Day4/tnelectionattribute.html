About the TN Election

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election gave a surprising result, as Vijay’s political party, TVK, became the single largest party by winning 108 seats. However, no party reached the majority mark of 118 seats, leading to a hung assembly in the state. The DMK won 59 seats, while AIADMK secured 47 seats.

Smaller parties like Congress, PMK, and IUML also won a few seats. After the results, political parties started discussions and alliance talks to form the new government in Tamil Nadu.

The Tamil Nadu Election 2026 is one of the most important political events in the state. People from different districts participate in voting to elect their representatives and choose the next government.

Major political parties campaign across Tamil Nadu by conducting public meetings, rallies, and awareness programs. The election focuses on important issues such as employment, education, development, infrastructure, agriculture, and welfare schemes.

Many young voters are also taking active interest in politics and voting for the future of the state. Political leaders travel to different constituencies to meet the public and share their promises and plans.

The Election Commission ensures that the voting process is conducted fairly and peacefully. Tamil Nadu elections always attract huge attention because of the strong political history and active participation of the people.

Election Result

According to recent 2026 election result reports, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) led by Vijay emerged as the biggest party in the Tamil Nadu Assembly election.

Approximate seat results (234 total seats):

  • TVK — Around 108 seats
  • DMK Alliance — Around 73 seats
  • AIADMK Alliance — Around 53 seats

Important points:

  • TVK became the single largest party but was slightly below the majority mark of 118 seats.
  • M. K. Stalin and DMK faced major losses.
  • This election was considered historic because it broke the long-time DMK vs AIADMK dominance in Tamil Nadu politics.
