Beautiful Forests

Forests are often called the lungs of the Earth because they provide oxygen and absorb carbon dioxide. Dense forests are home to thousands of plant species, birds, insects, and wild animals.

Forests also help maintain rainfall, prevent soil erosion, and support biodiversity. Many people visit forests to enjoy trekking, camping, photography, and peaceful natural surroundings.

Wonderful Waterfalls

Waterfalls are among the most breathtaking natural attractions in the world. The sound of flowing water and the cool atmosphere around waterfalls create a relaxing experience for visitors.

Many waterfalls are surrounded by green forests and mountains, making them perfect destinations for tourism and nature exploration.

Wildlife and Animals

Wildlife is an important part of nature and ecological balance. Animals, birds, reptiles, and insects play different roles in keeping ecosystems healthy and sustainable.

National parks and wildlife sanctuaries help protect endangered species and preserve natural habitats. Wildlife tourism also creates awareness about environmental conservation.
