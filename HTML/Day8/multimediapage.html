Beauty of Nature

Nature is one of the most beautiful gifts on Earth. Forests, mountains, rivers, waterfalls, oceans, and wildlife make our planet colorful and peaceful. Spending time in nature helps people feel relaxed, refreshed, and connected to the environment.

Protecting nature is important because it provides fresh air, clean water, food, and shelter for all living beings. Trees and plants also help maintain ecological balance and support life on Earth.
