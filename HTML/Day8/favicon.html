Understanding Favicon in HTML

A favicon is a small icon that appears on the browser tab beside the title of a webpage. It helps users easily identify a website when multiple tabs are open.

Favicons improve the appearance and branding of a website. Most websites use their company logo or a simple symbol as a favicon.

Advantages of Using a Favicon

  • Improves website identity
  • Makes browser tabs easy to recognize
  • Creates a professional look
  • Enhances user experience
